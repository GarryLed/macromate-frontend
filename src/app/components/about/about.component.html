About MacroMate

MacroMate is a personal nutrition and fitness tracking app built with Angular. It helps you stay on top of your health goals by logging daily meals, monitoring macronutrients, tracking your weight and progress over time.

Whether you're aiming to lose weight, build muscle, or maintain a balanced lifestyle, MacroMate gives you the tools to track:

  • Calories and macros (protein, carbs, fat)
  • Daily meals organized by meal type
  • Weight & water intake tracking
  • Historical logs with daily summaries
  • Custom goals for calories and macro ratios
